Family

Family is an important social organization and the basic unit of social life. It is the cradle of civic virtues.It is within the family that the child learns the meaning of cooperation, self-control, loyalty and sympathy. The process of socialization starts in the family and this prepares the child for a world outside his home. It is only enables him to identify himself as an individual but also prepares him to be an ideal citizen.
